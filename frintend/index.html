THE PHILOSOPHY
BEHIND SUBMAX

A system that integrates calisthenics skill development with proven powerlifting principles—creating a structured, honest approach to strength acquisition.

WHERE 99% FAIL

Most athletes spend months—often years—approaching calisthenics with a "more is better" mentality. More sets, more intensity, more fatigue. This mindset, rooted in "no pain, no gain," often leads not to progress, but to stagnation—plateaus, chronic fatigue, and preventable injury.

THE TRUTH

Strength is not built through excess, but through precision. It is a delicate process governed by the calculated balance of volume, frequency, and intensity. By training submaximally—intelligently managing fatigue, coordination, and output—we create the conditions for consistent, sustainable adaptation through undulating progression.

THE SOLUTION

After over six years of studying strength science, followed by years of real-world application across hundreds of athletes, Anton developed the SubMax system. A framework that integrates proven powerlifting principles with the unique demands of calisthenics skill acquisition.

THE RESULT

The result is a unified, adaptable system—engineered to build strength and mastery in complex calisthenics movements. A methodology that evolves with the individual, aligning with their structure, capacity, and goals—delivering results regardless of starting point.
